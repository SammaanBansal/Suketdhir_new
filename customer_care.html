RETURN POLICY

If you would like to return an item, please request a Return Authorization (RA) number within 10 days of receiving your order. Please note that we do not accept refunds for Fine Jewelry, but we allow exchange within the 10 day return window. You may request an RA number by emailing orders@maiyet.com with your order number and the reason for your return. Your item must be shipped to returns within 5 days of receiving your RA number. We will accept full-priced merchandise with their original packaging and tickets. Merchandise must be unused, unworn and in an unaltered condition. Footwear must be tried on carpeted surfaces only. Approved returns are free of charge. Refunds may only be issued in the same form as payment. Sale merchandise is final sale, refunds or exchanges will not be issued.
